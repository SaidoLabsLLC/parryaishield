Privacy Policy — ParryAI Chrome Extension

Effective Date: April 20, 2026 · Developer: Saido Labs LLC · Primary contributor: Jesse Crawford

1. Overview

ParryAI is a browser extension that protects you from prompt injection attacks when using AI assistants (ChatGPT, Claude, and Gemini). This policy explains what data the extension accesses, how it is processed, and what controls you have.

Core principle: your prompts never leave your browser. All detection runs locally inside the extension using a heuristic engine.

2. Data We Access and How It Is Processed

2.1 Prompt text (AI request bodies)

When you submit a message to ChatGPT, Claude, or Gemini, ParryAI intercepts the outgoing request at the browser level — before it is transmitted to the AI provider — and extracts your user-facing message text.

What happensDetail
Where it is processedEntirely on your device, inside the extension's service worker.
Is it transmitted externally?No. The text is passed to the local detection engine and immediately discarded after scoring.
Is the raw prompt text stored?No.
What is stored?Only the scan result metadata: threat score (0–1), severity label, category tag, source URL, and timestamp. Kept in chrome.storage.session (cleared when the browser closes) and capped at the 200 most recent entries.

2.2 Settings

The following settings are stored in chrome.storage.local (persistent, local to your device):

KeyPurpose
scanningEnabledGlobal on/off toggle
blockThresholdMinimum score to block a request (default 0.6)
providerChatGPTWhether ChatGPT scanning is enabled
providerClaudeWhether Claude scanning is enabled
providerGeminiWhether Gemini scanning is enabled

Settings data never leaves your device.

2.3 Session threat history

ParryAI maintains a rolling window of up to 200 scan results per browser session in chrome.storage.session. Each entry contains a timestamp, threat score and severity, category tag, source URL, and request source type (fetch or XHR). This history is displayed in the popup's threat timeline and is automatically erased when you close the browser. It is never uploaded or synced anywhere.

3. Data We Do NOT Collect

  • We do not log, transmit, or store your prompt text.
  • We do not collect your browsing history.
  • We do not use third-party analytics, telemetry, or crash-reporting services.
  • We do not track users across websites.
  • We do not share any data with third parties.

4. Permissions

PermissionWhy it is needed
storageSaves your settings locally and keeps a session-only threat history.
Host permissions (chatgpt.com, chat.openai.com, claude.ai, gemini.google.com)Injects the content script into these pages to monitor prompt submissions.

The extension does not request tabs, webRequest, notifications, cookies, history, or any other permission. The "View Details" button uses chrome.tabs.create, which is available to extensions without the tabs permission.

5. Third-Party Services

The extension's popup contains a "View Details" button that opens https://app.parryai.com/dashboard in a new tab. This is a standard tab navigation — no data is passed in the request. The web app is subject to its own privacy policy.

This release of ParryAI does not contain any API-key, account, or cloud-sync feature. Detection is entirely local.

6. Children's Privacy

ParryAI is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children.

7. Security

All detection processing occurs locally within the browser's extension sandbox. The extension uses Chrome Manifest V3, which enforces a strict Content Security Policy (no eval, no inline scripts), isolated execution contexts for content scripts, and a separate service worker with no persistent background state.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy as the extension evolves. Material changes will be noted in the extension's changelog. The effective date at the top of this document reflects the most recent revision.
