2. Description of Service

ParryAI is a browser extension that provides real-time detection of potentially harmful content in prompts submitted to AI chat services, including ChatGPT, Claude, and Google Gemini. The Extension intercepts outgoing prompts, analyzes them using a local detection engine, and either blocks the request or lets it through based on your configured threshold.
